ABOUT

Brahmapur Municipal Corporation has 8 (Eight) Kalyan Mandaps and 3 community centres at different locations of Brahmapur. It is mainly used as marriage or reception venues. Major mandaps are Nayapalli Kalyan Mandap, Sahidnagar Kalyan Mandap (Ground floor), Sahidnagar Kalyan Mandap (First floor), V.S.S Nagar Kalyan Mandap, Chandrasekharpur Kalyan Mandap, Badagada Kalyan Mandap, Dakhinchandi Kalyan Mandap, Unit-8 Community Centre, Shastri Nagar Community Centre. You can also avail Off season discounts "50% for Birthdayv/ Meeting / Satsang" between April, August, September and October). Also you can get 30% discount for Exhibition / Mela / Mina Bazar. Aiming for a better coverage of Kalyan Mandap in Bhubaneswar, the Bhubaneswar Municipal Corporation (BMC) has identified the locations for setting up 19 new Kalyan Mandap in across the city. Existing 8 Kalyan Mandaps & 3 community centres are not enough to meet the requirements, specially during the peak wedding seasons. The citizens were left with no choice but to book the private wedding halls at a much higher price. But with the identification of the land for the mandap, the BMC is now one step closer towards their construction. The BMC also has provisions to upgrade and modernise the existing seven kalyan mandaps.







Salient Features

The aesthetic design, modern amenities and prime location makes us an ideal choice for marriages, various festivities and celebrations. Features such as ample parking space, modern kitchen facilities, A/C Hall, A/C rooms for bride and groom, spacious dining hall, fully covered buffet space sets it apart.
